Transverse

Transverse is a 2D game designed to challenge the player’s hand-eye coordination as well as their memory. The player must get from Point A to Point B by navigating the player’s avatar (by raising or lowering their hand) through a series of treacherous pathways. Whenever the player’s avatar manages to come into contact with the hazardous landscape, the game resets and the player must start over.
